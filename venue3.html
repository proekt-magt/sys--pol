VENUE

The 1st International Workshop on Computational & Information Technologies for Risk-Informed Systems (CITRisk-2020) was originally planned to have a physical location (Kherson, Berislav Road, 24, Building No. 1 of Kherson National Technical University, Room 319(1)), but due to the worldwide coronavirus epidemy and the corresponding anti-epidemic restrictions established by the government, the physical arrival of participants from other cities and countries to Kherson in October 2020 is impossible, so the physical meeting should be cancelled. Since our publisher (CEUR) has temporarily allowed remote participation in workshops, CITRisk-2020 will be held online.
Thus, the workshop will be conducted remotely, in video conferencing format, using the Google Meet platform.
Links to Google Meet and the conference program will be available after October 10, 2020).
